## 2. Estimation Methodology

### 2.1 Approaches Used

- [ ] **Bottom-up estimation:** Decompose scope to work packages, estimate each
- [ ] **Analogous estimation:** Base on similar past engagements
- [ ] **Parametric estimation:** Use historical metrics (e.g., story points × velocity)
- [ ] **Three-point estimation:** Optimistic / Most likely / Pessimistic (PERT)

### 2.2 Estimation Assumptions

| # | Assumption | Impact if Wrong |
|---|-----------|----------------|
| 1 | All requirements are known at estimation time | ±20% on effort |
| 2 | Client SMEs available 20% of their time | ±15% on timeline |
