# Run & Evolve Phase

## Purpose

Operate the delivered solution, manage ongoing change, and continuously improve delivery capability and outcomes for the client.

## Guiding Principle

> The engagement is not complete at go-live. Value is realised through operation, adoption, and continuous improvement. Protect the delivery relationship through this phase.

## Key Questions

1. Is the solution operating as designed?
2. What improvements have been identified through operation?
3. Are BAU processes and teams fully transitioned?
4. What lessons should feed into future engagements?
5. Are SLAs and KPIs being met?

## Entry Criteria

- Solution accepted and in production
- Support model agreed and operational
- Transition complete

## Exit Criteria

- Lessons learned documented
- Knowledge transferred to client BAU teams
- Engagement formally closed
- Portfolio updated

## Sub-Phases

1. **Support Related Efforts** — Provide hypercare and BAU support
2. **Validate** — Confirm solution performance against acceptance criteria
3. **Transition** — Hand over to client BAU teams and formally close
