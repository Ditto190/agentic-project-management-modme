# Design & Implement Phase

## Purpose

Translate the agreed scope and vision into a detailed solution design, mobilise the delivery team, and execute against the plan. Manage quality, change control, and governance throughout.

## Guiding Principle

> Delivery discipline is the multiplier. A great design poorly executed delivers poor outcomes. Maintain plan integrity, communicate proactively, and manage change rigorously.

## Key Questions

1. Does our solution design fully address the agreed scope?
2. Are all workstreams aligned on the integrated delivery plan?
3. Are dependencies tracked and managed proactively?
4. Is the change control framework operational?
5. Are financial actuals tracking against budget?
6. Are governance structures functioning as designed?

## Entry Criteria

- SoW or contract signed
- Scope baseline approved
- Delivery team mobilised
- Governance framework operational

## Exit Criteria

- All in-scope deliverables produced and accepted
- UAT completed and signed off
- Transition plan agreed
- Lessons learned captured
- Financial close-out initiated

## Sub-Phases

1. **Define Solution Blueprint** — Detailed solution architecture and design
2. **Define Solution Strategy** — Delivery strategy, tooling, and approach decisions
3. **Define Delivery Mobilisation Approach** — Team, environments, and governance setup
4. **Obtain Delivery Centre Support** — Engage specialist practices or delivery centre resources
