# Work Planning & Dependency Management

## Description

Develop the integrated delivery plan, manage cross-workstream dependencies, and track progress against the plan.

## Deliverables

- [[DLV-008]] Mobilisation Plan (initial planning artefact)

## Key Questions

1. Is the integrated plan realistic given known dependencies?
2. Are all cross-workstream dependencies visible and owned?
3. What is the critical path and how do we protect it?
