# Estimation & Work Breakdown

## Description

Decompose the agreed scope into a Work Breakdown Structure (WBS) and produce effort, cost, and timeline estimates.

## Deliverables

- [[DLV-004]] Work Breakdown Structure
- [[DLV-012]] Estimation Model

## Key Questions

1. Have all WBS items been estimated to appropriate granularity?
2. What estimation method is appropriate for this scope and risk profile?
3. What is our confidence level and what contingency is needed?
