## Description

Establish financial tracking, forecasting, and reporting mechanisms for the engagement.

## Deliverables

## Key Questions

1. Are actuals being tracked against the SoW commercial model?
2. Is the EAC (Estimate at Completion) updated fortnightly?
3. Are any variances explained and escalated?
