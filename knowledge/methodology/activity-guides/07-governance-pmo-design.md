# Governance & PMO Design

## Description

Design the governance model, steering structures, reporting cadences, and PMO operating model for the engagement.

## Deliverables

- [[DLV-007]] Governance Framework

## Key Questions

1. Who makes which decisions at what level?
2. How will issues be escalated before they become blockers?
3. What reporting does the client expect and at what cadence?
