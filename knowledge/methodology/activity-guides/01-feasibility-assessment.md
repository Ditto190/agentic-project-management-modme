# Feasibility & Opportunity Assessment

## Description

Evaluate the viability, strategic fit, and commercial opportunity of a potential engagement before committing delivery resources.

## Responsibilities

- Conduct market and client context research
- Assess technical and organisational feasibility
- Identify key risks and unknowns at opportunity stage
- Produce go/no-go recommendation

## Deliverables

- [[DLV-001]] Feasibility Assessment Report

## Applied In Scope

- Proposal
- RFP Response
- Internal Business Case

## Key Questions

1. Is the opportunity technically feasible with our current capabilities?
2. Is the commercial model viable (margin, risk, payment terms)?
3. Are the client's expectations achievable within their stated constraints?
4. What are the top 3 risks if we proceed?
5. What would a successful outcome look like in 12 months?

## Common Pitfalls

- Over-promising at opportunity stage to win the deal
- Insufficient technical assessment before committing to fixed price
- Missing key assumptions that should be contractual obligations
- Failing to document the go/no-go rationale (important for lessons learned)
