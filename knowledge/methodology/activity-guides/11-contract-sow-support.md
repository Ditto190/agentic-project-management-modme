# Contract & SOW Drafting Support

## Description

Support legal and commercial teams in drafting, reviewing, and negotiating the Statement of Work and Master Services Agreement.

## Deliverables

- [[DLV-005]] Statement of Work

## Key Questions

1. Does the SoW accurately reflect the agreed scope, deliverables, and commercial model?
2. Are client responsibilities explicitly stated?
3. Are all key assumptions embedded as contractual obligations?

## Key Principle

> By the time of contract signing, the contract should fully align with the solution plan, financials, and importantly, client expectations.
