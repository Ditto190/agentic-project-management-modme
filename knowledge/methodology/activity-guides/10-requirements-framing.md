# Requirements Framing (Pre-Signature)

## Description

Frame high-level requirements to inform scope definition, estimation, and SoW drafting prior to contract signature.

## Deliverables

- [[DLV-010]] Requirements Plan

## Key Questions

1. Are requirements at the right level of detail to support estimation?
2. Have non-functional requirements (performance, security) been captured?
3. Are requirements traceable back to client objectives?
