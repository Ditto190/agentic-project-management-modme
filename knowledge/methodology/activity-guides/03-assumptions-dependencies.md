# Assumptions & Dependencies Logging

## Description

Capture, track, and validate all assumptions and dependencies that underpin the delivery plan and commercial model.

## Deliverables

- [[DLV-014]] Assumptions Log

## Key Questions

1. What are we assuming to be true that we haven't yet confirmed?
2. What external factors must occur for our plan to succeed?
3. Which assumptions have the highest impact if they prove false?

## Common Pitfalls

- Treating assumptions as facts in the plan
- Not logging assumptions at all until something goes wrong
- Failing to validate assumptions before they become risks
