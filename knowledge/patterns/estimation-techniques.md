# Estimation Techniques

## Techniques

| Technique | When to Use | Confidence |
|-----------|------------|-----------|
| Bottom-Up | Scope well-defined, WBS complete | High (±10-15%) |
| Analogous | Early stage, similar past engagement available | Medium (±25-35%) |
| Three-Point (PERT) | High uncertainty; `E = (O + 4M + P) / 6` | Medium with range |
| Parametric | Repeatable work with velocity data | Medium-High |

## Contingency

| Confidence | Recommended Contingency |
|------------|------------------------|
| High | 10-15% |
| Medium | 20-25% |
| Low | 30-40% |
